Why change?

Since studying animation at University, I have had a growing interest in web development but have felt that I was not smart enough to grasp the concepts. After I graduated, I was fortunate enough to work at Tree Top Films where I had the opportunity to work as part of a team and hone my design and animation skills in an exciting team. While at Tree Top Films, my interest in web development grew and I was able to dip my toe into the development waters with thanks to After Effects expressions. This growing fire inside eventually led me to leave Tree Top Films and work as a freelancer while I studied full stack software development at Code Institute. Now that I have built a strong, foundational programming knowledge I am confident to combine my technical and creative skills in the tech industry.
